*BSD integrated releases

The following platforms have KAME code integrated. If you are a normal user who just would like to try out IPv6 or IPsec (and your main interest is not in IPv6/IPsec protocol stack development), grab the latest releases from the following locations:
NetBSD 1.5 and beyond: http://www.netbsd.org/
OpenBSD 2.7 and beyond: http://www.openbsd.org/
BSDI BSD/OS 4.2 and beyond: http://www.bsdi.com/
FreeBSD 4.0 and beyond: http://www.freebsd.org/
