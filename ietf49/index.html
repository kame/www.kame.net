IETF49 IPv6 network instructions

IPv6 global address prefix?
2001:0240:00ff:0000::/64 (802.11 wireless)
2001:0240:00ff:0001::/64 (wired ethernet in laptop term room - from Monday)
How to configure my notebook PC?
Use IPv6 stateless address autoconfiguration (RFC2462). DHCPv6 server is not provided at this moment.
How do I get static IPv6 address?
We do not do that. Autoconfigure it.
IPv6 transport-ready nameservers?
3ffe:501:4819::42
3ffe:1::c620:242, 198.32.2.66 (z.ip6.int)
3ffe:50e::1 (y.ip6.int)
IPv6-to-IPv4 translators?
  • Set your nameserver to 2001:200:ff:0::42, or 207.137.72.36.
  • Your tcp traffic to 2001:0240:00ff:ffff::ipv4addr will be relayed to ipv4addr.
  • Supported ports are: telnet ftp ssh pop3 http icb printer smtp.
  • Need additional tcp port to be translated? Contact ietf49@kame.net.
Which network segments are IPv6 ready?
Laptop term room, and 802.11 wireless segment.
Service time?
Whenever terminal cluster is running. Hopefully 24hours, starting from Saturday and terminating around Friday noon.
Connectivity to the outside world?
RFC2893 tunnel to IIJ equipment in Palo Alto, California (PAIX). IPv4 traceroute for tunnelled portion.
Router equipment?
The latest KAME stack, running on top of BSDI BSD/OS 3.1. Hardware: Digital HiNote Ultra II LTS5150.
Any other questions?
Do not hesitate to contact ietf49@kame.net.
